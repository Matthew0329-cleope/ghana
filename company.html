About TeleMed Ghana

Leading healthcare innovation since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, TeleMed Ghana emerged as a response to the healthcare challenges faced by remote communities across Ghana. Our journey began when a team of medical professionals, technologists, and entrepreneurs came together with a shared vision of making quality healthcare accessible to all Ghanaians, regardless of their location.

Starting with a small pilot program in the Northern Region, we've now expanded our operations nationwide, connecting patients in rural areas with specialists in major urban centers through our groundbreaking 3D telemedicine platform.

Backed by international partners and the Ministry of Health, TeleMed Ghana has become a model for healthcare innovation across West Africa, demonstrating how technology can bridge geographic divides to deliver life-saving medical care.

M Our Mission

To transform healthcare delivery in Ghana by leveraging cutting-edge 3D telemedicine technology to connect patients in underserved areas with quality medical expertise, reducing healthcare disparities and improving health outcomes for all Ghanaians.

We are committed to making specialized healthcare accessible to every citizen regardless of geographic location, socioeconomic status, or infrastructure limitations.

V Our Vision

To create a Ghana where every citizen has equal access to quality healthcare services through innovative technology solutions.

We envision a future where distance is no longer a barrier to receiving expert medical care, where preventable conditions are diagnosed early, and where health outcomes are equitable across urban and rural communities.

Our ultimate goal is to develop a model that can be replicated across Africa, contributing to continent-wide healthcare transformation.

Success Stories

Transforming Care in Tamale

In the Northern Region of Ghana, the Tamale Community Health Center was struggling to provide specialized care to its 50,000 residents. After implementing our 3D telemedicine system, they now connect with specialists from Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital daily, leading to a 40% improvement in accurate diagnoses and timely treatments.

Emergency Response in Rural Western Region

When a bus accident injured 23 people in a remote village, local healthcare workers used our platform to consult with trauma specialists in Accra. The immediate virtual assessments helped prioritize treatment, stabilize patients, and coordinate air evacuations for critical cases, saving at least 7 lives according to emergency responders.

Childhood Health Initiative

Our partnership with UNICEF has brought specialized pediatric care to 35 underserved communities, screening over 15,000 children for developmental issues and chronic conditions. Early interventions facilitated through our platform have improved outcomes for hundreds of children who would otherwise have gone undiagnosed.
